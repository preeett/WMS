Weather Monitoring Station

A weather monitoring station is a device that collects data related to the atmosphere, temperature, humidity, and more. It is especially useful in physics, where students can study weather patterns, pressure systems, and air quality. Monitoring atmospheric conditions is critical in understanding various physical phenomena such as heat transfer, fluid dynamics, and gas laws.
